Meet Our Founder: Dr. Kharika Devi Parajuli

Dr. Parajuli is a highly accomplished mathematics teacher educator with over 24 years of experience. She holds a Ph.D. in Mathematics Education from Tribhuvan University (2022) and an M.Phil. from Kathmandu University (2017).

Her doctoral research, "Connecting Vedic, Ethno, and School Mathematics," explores how local cultural knowledge and artifacts can be transformed into powerful pedagogical designs. She has published extensively in national and international journals and contributed a chapter to a prestigious Springer book on ethnomathematics.

  • Founder & Director, Global Academy of Education Research
  • Executive Member, Nepal Mathematical Society (Gandaki Province)
  • Director, Centre for Innovation, Research, Development & Training (CIRDT)
  • Life Member, Women of Nepal in Mathematical Sciences (WoNiMS)

हाम्री संस्थापक: डा. खरीका देवी पराजुली

डा. पराजुली २४ वर्षभन्दा बढी अनुभव भएकी एक प्रख्यात गणित शिक्षक शिक्षिका हुनुहुन्छ। उहाँले त्रिभुवन विश्वविद्यालयबाट गणित शिक्षामा पीएच.डी. (२०२२) र काठमाडौं विश्वविद्यालयबाट एम.फिल. (२०१७) प्राप्त गर्नुभएको छ।

उहाँको अनुसन्धान "Connecting Vedic, Ethno, and School Mathematics" ले स्थानीय सांस्कृतिक ज्ञान र कलाकृतिहरूलाई शक्तिशाली शैक्षिक डिजाइनमा कसरी रूपान्तरण गर्न सकिन्छ भन्ने अन्वेषण गर्दछ। उहाँले राष्ट्रिय र अन्तर्राष्ट्रिय जर्नलहरूमा व्यापक रूपमा प्रकाशित गर्नुभएको छ र एथनोम्याथमेटिक्समा Springer पुस्तकमा अध्याय योगदान गर्नुभएको छ।

  • संस्थापक तथा निर्देशक, ग्लोबल एकेडेमी अफ एजुकेसन रिसर्च

🧩 The N‑MELODY Framework

A proprietary pedagogical model developed by Dr. Parajuli that integrates Critical Pedagogy, Reflective Practice, and Transformative Education to create socially just, culturally relevant, and deeply engaging mathematics learning experiences.

  • N – Nepal / Numeracy / Nurturing
  • M – Mathematics / Modelling
  • E – Ethnomathematics / Engagement
  • L – Learning / Local Knowledge
  • O – Open Lab / Observation
  • D – Development / Diversity
  • Y – Yoga (Harmony) / Youth
Signature Framework
