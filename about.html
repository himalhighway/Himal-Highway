Our Story

Global Academy of Education Research Private Limited was established with a single, powerful mission: to advance mathematics education through rigorous research, innovative teacher training, and culturally responsive pedagogy. We believe that mathematics is not just a subject—it is a way of understanding the world, deeply connected to culture, art, and everyday life.

Our work is grounded in Ethnomathematics—the study of mathematical practices embedded in cultural traditions, artifacts, and lived experiences. By connecting school mathematics with indigenous knowledge systems (Rekhi, Mandap, temple arts, handicrafts), we make learning more meaningful, accessible, and socially just for all students.

Founded by Dr. Kharika Devi Parajuli—a distinguished mathematics teacher educator with over 24 years of experience—the Academy bridges the gap between traditional cultural knowledge and modern educational practice. We work with schools, universities, government agencies, and international development partners to design and implement transformative educational initiatives.

हाम्रो कथा

ग्लोबल एकेडेमी अफ एजुकेसन रिसर्च प्राइभेट लिमिटेड एउटा शक्तिशाली मिसनका साथ स्थापित गरिएको थियो: कठोर अनुसन्धान, नवीन शिक्षक तालिम र सांस्कृतिक-उत्तरदायी शिक्षाशास्त्र मार्फत गणित शिक्षाको उन्नति गर्ने। हाम्रो मान्यता छ: गणित केवल एक विषय मात्र होइन—यो संसार बुझ्ने एउटा तरिका हो, जुन संस्कृति, कला र दैनिक जीवनसँग गहिरो रूपमा जोडिएको छ।

हाम्रो कार्य नृजातीय गणित (Ethnomathematics) मा आधारित छ—सांस्कृतिक परम्पराहरू, कलाकृतिहरू र जीवन अनुभवहरूमा गाडिएको गणितीय अभ्यासहरूको अध्ययन। स्कूल गणितलाई आदिवासी ज्ञान प्रणालीहरू (रेखी, मण्डप, मन्दिर कलाहरू, हस्तकलाहरू) सँग जोडेर, हामी सबै विद्यार्थीहरूका लागि सिकाइलाई अझ अर्थपूर्ण, सुलभ र सामाजिक रूपमा न्यायसंगत बनाउँछौं।

डा. खरीका देवी पराजुली—२४ वर्षभन्दा बढी अनुभव भएकी एक प्रख्यात गणित शिक्षक शिक्षिका—द्वारा स्थापित, एकेडेमीले परम्परागत सांस्कृतिक ज्ञान र आधुनिक शैक्षिक अभ्यासबीचको सेतुको रूपमा काम गर्दछ। हामी विद्यालय, विश्वविद्यालय, सरकारी निकाय र अन्तर्राष्ट्रिय विकास साझेदारहरूसँग मिलेर परिवर्तनकारी शैक्षिक पहलहरूको डिजाइन र कार्यान्वयन गर्दछौं।

About Dr. Kharika Devi Parajuli

Dr. Parajuli is a highly accomplished mathematics teacher educator with over 24 years of professional experience in mathematics education. She has served as Head of the Mathematics Department in a private secondary school, senior teacher in public secondary schools, academic trainer, and researcher.

Her academic journey includes a Ph.D. in Mathematics Education from Tribhuvan University (2022), an M.Phil. in Mathematics Education from Kathmandu University (2017), and a Master’s degree from Tribhuvan University (2006). Her doctoral research, "Connecting Vedic, Ethno, and School Mathematics," explores how local cultural knowledge and artifacts can be systematically transformed into powerful pedagogical designs.

Ph.D. (Mathematics Education) Tribhuvan University, 2022
M.Phil. (Mathematics Education) Kathmandu University, 2017
M.Ed. (Mathematics Education) Tribhuvan University, 2006
B.Ed. (Mathematics Education) Tribhuvan University, 2001

Professional Leadership: Dr. Parajuli serves as Executive Member of the Nepal Mathematical Society (Gandaki Province), Director of the Centre for Innovation, Research, Development & Training (CIRDT), Treasurer of the Education Society Nepal, and Life Member of Women of Nepal in Mathematical Sciences (WoNiMS).

She has published extensively in national and international journals and contributed a chapter to a prestigious Springer book on ethnomathematics. She has presented at conferences in India, the Philippines, and virtually with global scholars including Prof. Milton Rosa and Prof. Daniel Orey.
